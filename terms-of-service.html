Introduction

Welcome to Analysts24x7. By accessing or using our services, you agree to comply with and be bound by the following terms and conditions.

Services Provided

Analysts24x7 offers data analysis and support services tailored to various industries. Services include internet research, data wrangling, visualization, audio/video editing, descriptive analytics, photo editing, predictive analytics and more.

User Responsibilities

Users must provide accurate and complete information when using our services. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account information and for all activities that occur under your account.

Service Availability

While Analysts24x7 strives to provide continuous service, we do not guarantee 24x7 availability of our analysts. In case of unavailability, work may be transferred to another qualified analyst.

Escalation Feature

Users can escalate any work through our platform. Our Tier 3 Service Engineers will handle escalations, working in different time zones to ensure timely resolution or reassignment to another analyst.

AI Integration

We may integrate AI functionalities, such as APIs connecting to ChatGPT, to enhance our services. These integrations will be introduced after ensuring the core functionalities of our product are stable.
